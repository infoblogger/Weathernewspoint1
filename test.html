Why should we be paying close attention to the forecast for weather?

For me, these are the most important reasons why you have to follow the forecast for the weather and why you should have a weather station in your home even if it's not the case that you have a passion for science in meteorology:

Plan your activities. Do you plan to do something? Do you know if it's going to rainand at what hour, or if the forecast is cold? Perhaps some knowledge would allow you to optimize your time and activities.

- Choose the right clothes. No one likes being cold. It's a bad way to ruin our day. You could also get wet because we did not take an umbrella to the office in the morning.

- Knowledge of our limits. When we keep track of the temperature and the weather We can learn the limits of our thermal capabilities and when we need to wrap up or remove our clothes.

- Save on cooling and heating.To many people it may seem absurd however, if you are able how to control and understand the perfect temperature inside as well as the way outside temperatures affect the inside of your house I am confident that you can significantly improve the utilization of energy to control it. If you also have an expectation of a sudden change in the weather, you could take preventive measures and make more efficient use. For instance, if you know in advance that a cold storm is coming, you can profit from the high outside temperature before the cold storm to increase the temperature inside by making use of less energy since it is not so cold. When the cold comes, you've already heated the house. This does not mean that you will save money in the long run, but by implementing tiny optimizations, you could save money over the course of time.

Be careful when talking on the subject of weather. If you know how the meteorology in your locale functions, you'll be able speak to guidelines of how severe the meteorology is acting.
